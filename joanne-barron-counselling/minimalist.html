Welcome

I believe that healing happens in relationship—with ourselves, with others, and with the wisdom of our own bodies. My work is grounded in deep curiosity, attunement, and a non-judgmental presence that creates space for authentic exploration and transformation.

Whether you're navigating relationship challenges, seeking to understand patterns that no longer serve you, or looking to reconnect with your body's innate wisdom, I'm here to walk alongside you in that journey.

My Approach

I work from a place of genuine curiosity about your unique experience. Rather than offering prescriptive solutions, I create a safe, attuned space where you can explore what's present for you—emotionally, somatically, and relationally.

What You Can Expect

  • A deeply curious and attuned presence
  • Non-judgmental exploration of your experience
  • Integration of somatic and relational awareness
  • Insightful reflections that honor your wisdom
  • A collaborative therapeutic relationship
  • Respect for your unique pace and process

My work integrates somatic practices, breathwork, and nervous system regulation with traditional talk therapy. I believe our bodies hold profound wisdom, and learning to listen to and work with our nervous system can be deeply transformative.

Areas of Focus

Couples Counselling

Supporting partners to deepen connection, navigate conflict with greater ease, and understand the patterns that emerge between them. I help couples move beyond reactive cycles toward genuine understanding and intimacy.

Somatic Work

Exploring the wisdom of the body and learning to recognize and respond to bodily sensations, tensions, and stored experiences. This work helps integrate mind and body for deeper healing.

Breathwork

Using conscious breathing practices to access deeper states of awareness, release held tension, and regulate the nervous system. Breathwork can be a powerful gateway to transformation.

Nervous System Regulation

Learning to understand and work with your nervous system's responses. This approach helps build capacity for emotional regulation, resilience, and a greater sense of safety in your body.
