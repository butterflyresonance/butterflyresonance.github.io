Welcome

"In the safety of connection, we can finally begin to feel, to heal, and to come home to ourselves."

Whether you're navigating relationship challenges, reconnecting with your body, or seeking to understand patterns that feel stuck, I'm here to offer a warm, attuned presence on your journey.

What to Expect

  • A safe, welcoming space where all of your feelings are honored
  • Gentle exploration that respects your pace and comfort
  • Integration of body awareness with emotional processing
  • Supportive guidance without prescriptive solutions
  • A collaborative relationship built on trust and respect

I work with both mind and body, integrating somatic practices, breathwork, and nervous system awareness with compassionate dialogue. Our bodies hold so much wisdom—learning to listen to and befriend them can be profoundly healing.

Areas I Work With

Couples Counselling

Supporting partners to reconnect with tenderness and understanding. Together, we explore patterns of disconnection and cultivate new ways of being together that honor both individuals and the relationship.

Somatic Work

Gently exploring the messages your body holds—the tensions, sensations, and memories stored in your physical self. This embodied approach helps integrate all aspects of your experience for deeper healing.

Breathwork

Using the gentle power of conscious breathing to calm your nervous system, release what's been held, and access deeper states of peace and awareness. Breath is a bridge to your inner world.

Nervous System Regulation

Learning to understand and nurture your nervous system. This foundational work helps you build capacity for calm, develop resilience, and create a greater sense of safety and ease in your body and life.
