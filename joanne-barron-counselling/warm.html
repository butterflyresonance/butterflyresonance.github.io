A Space for Deep Listening

"Healing happens in relationship—with ourselves, with others, and with the profound intelligence of our bodies."

I work from a place of deep curiosity and attunement, creating a space where you can explore what's truly present for you. Whether you're navigating the complexities of intimate relationships, seeking to understand patterns that no longer serve you, or yearning to reconnect with your body's inherent wisdom, I'm here to walk alongside you.

My approach is grounded in the belief that you already possess everything you need for healing. My role is to offer a safe, non-judgmental presence where that wisdom can emerge and be honored.

How I Work

Rather than offering prescriptive solutions, I create an environment of genuine curiosity about your unique experience. This is a collaborative exploration—one where your insights, your body's signals, and your relational patterns are all welcomed and honored.

Areas of Focus

Couples Counselling

Supporting partners to move beyond reactive patterns toward genuine intimacy and understanding. Together, we explore the dance between you—the unspoken needs, the protective strategies, and the longing for true connection.

Somatic Work

Learning to listen to the language of your body—the sensations, tensions, and stored experiences that shape how you move through the world. This embodied approach integrates mind and body for deeper healing.

Breathwork

Using the power of conscious breathing to access deeper states of awareness, release what's been held, and come home to yourself. Breath is a bridge between the conscious and unconscious, the mind and the body.

Nervous System Regulation

Understanding and working with your nervous system's patterns and responses. This foundational work builds capacity for presence, resilience, and a deeper sense of safety in your own body.
